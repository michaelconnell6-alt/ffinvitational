The Rulebook

Tournament Format

These are the official rules of the ForeFathers Invitational. Ignorance is not a defense.

1
Net Stroke PlayScore minus course handicap. Best net total wins.
2
$150 Buy-InAll players must pay to play.
3
Payout Structure 1st — $1,250 + Champion Stein
2nd — $600
3rd — $350
4th — $200
4
OB Stakes OptionPlay 4 in the fairway at point of entry, or replay 3 from the tee.
5
Unmarked WoodsAll unmarked wooded areas are played as hazards.
6
Ball in the HoleAlways. No gimmes, no exceptions.
7
Pink Iron CoversLast place net each day must wear pink iron covers the following round — on after every single shot.
8
Rounds 1 & 2 PairingsRandomly generated so everyone gets to know each other.
9
Rounds 3 & 4 PairingsSplit by standings. Contenders together, the rest sort themselves out.
10
Relegation SpeechLast place after the final round must deliver a speech on why they should not be relegated from the trip.
11
Gambling Within GroupsNot only allowed — encouraged.
12
Triple Bogey MaxApplies for the first three days. Pick up and move on.
